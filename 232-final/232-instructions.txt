## MC 232 Final Exam - Instructions

Create the page shown in the file "232-final-comp.png" to the best of your abilities. If you're not sure how to accomplish something, give it your best shot. Effort counts for this exercise!

Please make all links point to "#"... ie: <a href="#">Link Text Here</a>

Here is roughly the breakdown of points:

HTML - 30%
 - Is it semantically structured?
 - Is it formatted cleanly?
 - Does it validate at the W3C validator?

CSS - 40%
 - Is it formatted cleanly?
 - Does it faithfully re-create the layout?
 - Are all colors correctly placed?
 - Are there any errors that prevent it from working correctly?

IMAGES - 10%
 - Are images resized correctly?
 - Are they displayed correctly in the page?

OVERALL FIDELITY - 20%
 - How closely does your work resemble the comp provided?
